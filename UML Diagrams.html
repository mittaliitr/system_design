Behavioral Diagrams

  • Activity Diagram: Flowchart-like diagram representing the flow of activities or operations in a system, useful for modeling workflows and business processes.
  • State Machine Diagram: Depicts the states of an object and transitions between those states, modeling dynamic behavior.

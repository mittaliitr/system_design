LLD Interview Problems

Common LLD interview problems test your ability to design robust, maintainable, and scalable systems:

Machine/Device Design

  • Parking Lot: Design classes and interactions for managing parking spaces, vehicles, and payments.
  • Vending Machine: Model item selection, payment, and dispensing logic.
  • Coffee Vending Machine: Similar to vending machine, but with beverage-specific logic.
  • ATM: Simulate transactions, account management, and cash dispensing.
  • Elevator System: Model elevators, requests, and scheduling.
  • Traffic Signal Control System: Design logic for managing traffic lights and their states.

Games

  • Tic Tac Toe: Model the board, players, and game rules.
  • Chess Game: Design classes for pieces, board, moves, and rules.
  • Snake and Ladder Game: Simulate board, players, dice, and movement logic.

Social & Communication

  • Stack Overflow: Model users, questions, answers, comments, and voting.
  • LinkedIn: Design classes for users, connections, messaging, and feeds.
  • Social Networking Service: Model user profiles, posts, relationships, and notifications.

Finance & Commerce

  • Digital Wallet Service: Handle accounts, transactions, and balances.
  • Online Auction System: Model bidding, item listing, and winner selection.
  • Online Stock Brokerage System: Simulate stock trading, portfolios, and order management.
  • Online Shopping Service: Design for product catalogs, carts, orders, and payments.

Media & Entertainment

  • Music Streaming Service: Model playlists, tracks, users, and streaming logic.
  • Movie Ticket Booking System: Handle seat selection, booking, and payments.
  • Concert Ticket Booking System: Similar to movie booking, with event-specific features.

Transport

  • Car Rental System: Model cars, reservations, and billing.
  • Ride-Sharing Service: Design matching, ride management, and payments.
  • Airline Management System: Simulate flights, bookings, and ticketing.

Hospitality

  • Hotel Management System: Manage rooms, bookings, and customers.
  • Restaurant Management System: Handle tables, orders, and billing.

Utilities

  • Logging Framework: Design classes for logging messages with different levels and outputs.
  • Task Management System: Model tasks, users, statuses, and notifications.
  • LRU Cache: Implement a cache with least-recently-used eviction policy.
  • Pub-Sub System: Design publisher, subscriber, and message dispatch logic.

Education

  • Course Registration System: Manage courses, students, and enrollments.
  • Library Management System: Handle books, users, and borrowing logic.

Other

  • Splitwise: Model expense splitting and settlements among friends.
  • CricInfo: Simulate cricket match data, players, and stats.
  • Food Delivery Service: Design order management, delivery tracking, and payments.
